E-Portfolio - Felipe Camacho Hurtado

Hello Everyone!

My name is Felipe Camacho, Geoscientist from Bogotá (Colombia). Thanks for the support of the Eramus + Programme of the European Union,  I'm currently enrrolled in the "Copernicus Master in Digital Earth" Master Programme. ​Currently, I'm located in Salzburg (Austria) but in the winter of 2023, I will move to Olomouc (Czech Republic).

I'm interested in the application of Remote Sensing and GIS techniques for developing environmental and utility management solutions, and cartography as communication media with the communities. 

I hope you can get to know me a little bit through this webpage. Please, feel free to contact me.
